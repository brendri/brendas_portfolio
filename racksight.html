Summary

About RackSight

RackSight is a mobile app that offers personalized recommendations, featuring a scanning tool that provides detailed information about gym equipment and videos for visual guidance. Additionally, its social feed creates a supportive gym community where users can share content, ask questions, and engage in workouts together.

Problem

There is a lack of accessible, organized content for beginners to effectively learn and implement proper workout techniques. Fitness apps often fall short of providing the specific features needed, making it difficult to track progress and understand machine instructions, leaving users frustrated and uncertain.

Our Solution

To address this, I've developed RackSight—a mobile app that provides personalized guidance, detailed machine scanning, and a supportive social community to help users seamlessly navigate their fitness journeys.

Investigating the Problem

Started with an Interview Analysis

I conducted brief interviews with 2-3 individuals who showcased their gym styles in person, which helped me gain a deeper understanding of the challenges they faced as both beginners and intermediate gym-goers.


In my analysis, I found some common key themes and insights, such as…

  • Accessibility and Affordability: The cost of fitness apps and subscriptions poses a financial barrier, limiting access to resources for all users.
  • Personalized Approaches: Without guidance, users struggle to understand what works best for their fitness goals, contributing to inconsistency.
  • Instructional Support: Users find machine instructions unhelpful, and some machines lack instructions altogether, discouraging exploration and motivation..
  • Tracker: There is a lack of consistency in tracking everyday activities when working out, leading to stagnation and random workouts.

Creating Personas

I selected 2 individuals from my brief interviews, representing both beginner and intermediate levels. The insights gained from these interviews allowed me to synthesize key takeaways into two representative personas, Amanda Lynn and Juan Estrada, each reflecting their distinct challenges and unique goals.

Exploring Scenarios

  1. Amanda Lynn struggles to establish a consistent gym routine due to her busy college schedule and lack of experience with machine-based equipment.
    RackSight helps Amanda by providing personalized workout recommendations, tracking her progress, offering machine scanning for proper form, and connecting her with a supportive social community for guidance and motivation.
  2. Juan Estrada struggles with maintaining a consistent workout schedule, tracking his progress, and effectively using gym machines due to a lack of clear instructions and understanding of their capabilities.
    RackSight supports Juan by helping him maintain a consistent workout routine with detailed tracking of his progress. The app provides machine scanning to better understand each machine's capabilities and offers personalized guidance to enhance his fitness journey.

Shaping the Design

User Flows

After collecting all the data, paper design flows were used for quick iteration, which were later transformed into more detailed digital versions with multiple screens and interactions. These user flows helped outline the user's journey through the product, ensuring smooth transitions between lo-fi and hi-fi prototyping.

Design System

The design system for RackSight began by coordinating color contrasts and text sizes to effectively communicate the app's purpose. The focus was on creating a progression of interactive elements that highlighted key details such as icons and controls.

Key Design Features

Machine Scanner

Effortlessly scans gym equipment to identify targeted muscles, showcasing detailed information and providing visual guidance for proper usage.

Intuitive Search Bar

Easily navigates with keywords like "cable" to provide information and demonstrations on various ways to use cable machines effectively.

Customized Homepage

Delivers a personalized experience based on onboarding preferences, redirecting users to an information page that showcases diverse workout concepts and detailed guidance.
